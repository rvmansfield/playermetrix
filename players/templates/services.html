Our Services

Player Metrics Testing Events

Our core offering is captruing player data through a series of tests using technology to get accurate measurements. Each player is measured on metrics including the 60 yard dash, IF/OF throwing velocity, hitting velocity, pitch velocity, height/weight and more. After data has been captured the information is sent to the player/coaches and posted online where it can be shared.

Recruiting Video

A recruiting video is an essential tool in the process of being seen by coaches and scouts. Athletes should highlight their best skills in order to increase their visibility with coaches.

Let us provide our expertise and services to ensure your video gets noticed.

  • You can provide the video content or we can schedule a time to do the recording.
  • We will edit the video, add graphics and player's information.
  • Once it is ready you can send to coaches, post on social media, etc.
